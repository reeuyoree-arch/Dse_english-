進行中挑戰 (雙欄對應)：
Inversion (倒裝句結構) Core Structure

📌 Structure Formula

Negative Adverb (e.g., Seldom, Rarely, Not only) + Auxiliary Verb (助動詞) + Subject + Main Verb

📝 Example Sentence

"Not only did he pass the exam, but he also secured a scholarship."

⚠️

Common Mistakes

忘記將助動詞（did/does/had）提至主語前方，寫成 Not only he passed...

Relative Clauses (關係從句進階用法) Sentence Variety

📌 Structure Formula

Noun + Preposition (e.g., in which, through which) + Relative Pronoun

📝 Example Sentence

"The adversity through which she persevered defined her authentic character."

⚠️

Common Mistakes

介系詞選用錯誤，需搭配先行詞動詞的固定搭配用法。
